Compute needs to be constructed and manufactured using globally distributed parts. Compute needs to be cooled, and needs humans and electricity to operate. These are fairly straightforward facts. Triples can be written in many forms, most of which are optimized for machines (W3C 2014b). We are optimizing our analysis for human cognition, though, so using emoji is a plus.
